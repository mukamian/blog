A lot has changed since I saw you last
The dream I had you in, now have a different cast
Still, here I am underneath the light from past
Scratch of pencil, still tears us apart
I want you to see who I have become now
But the distance seems deep, I don’t know-how
I remember what led us into these two plains
But those are just moments with time and place
I know, things would never be the same
And now the silence is all that remains
You must know that those songs still have your name
Still those words struggle when out is what they aim
Swelling every part of mine for just last drop that is left
But you know, there’s nothing left.
