That glare in her eyes in which I kept on staring
Waiting, for a drop to just show some wearing
“Do you want to tell me something?” praying for just a whisper
A nod in denial made the silence crisper
With each thought rushing, my feet felt cold
Wishing for something to be missing or just sometime to hold
Smiling was best I could do just to hide my heart racking
“Can you give me a hug?” Her toughness was cracking
“You know, I can’t”, pulling myself together I broke her hard
But I won’t let her get any more scared
Betraying her eyes, a drop set its sail
I was distant but still see her pale
Stretched arms shivered, squeezing in on the gap that resides
All left a humming song, I can’t decide
