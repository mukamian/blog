Someday rose my head to see what goes around
A race I was in but nowhere neither in nor out
Heard no cheer no applause only screams and cries surround
I wasn’t sleeping yet but still, last when I found
No mentions of the reason nor facts, no one around to hear
But all I know I don’t belong the place but still, I’m here
Word, I wrote down are the only thing which never messes around
Maybe not recognized or even been heard in this crowd
But at least I got them out, no matter for some it just howls
Always wished to write a story that has success as they allowed
But my mistake, my faults which I guess are too loud
Yeah! I accept the fact I don’t concentrate at my faults
And might my ignorance to the fact add my mess some salt
But the story is also about the one who survives
Someone how remains the way he is staying for lives
Don’t think this is to write but still, I rhymes
Thinking someone around could add this thought to elongate these hymns
