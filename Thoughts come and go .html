She told me we were friend and thus locked
But friends, Girl, friends do talk.
They talk about fault, they talk about mistakes,
They take you down when you do thing, they don’t like at any sake
They do not keep wishing of an opportunity to by your side
They stand really tall with no regrets or fear of any kind
They laugh seeing each other, at each other hear whatever they got
And do not require anybody else to look at and deliver their thoughts
Do you know, all that left between us now is a certainly a fear
You fear, of me as I make everything that turns to be mess, I hear
And I fear of the same, cause situations made believe rare
You once asked me why didn’t I give up on you till how long I would bear
Girl I already had given up and the left is just unspoken words which is still not clear
The mug on my table resembles of this relation we have, hazy and underneath a layer
