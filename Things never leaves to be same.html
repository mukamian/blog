While seeing the world pacing around
With the increasing roar of the engine and crowd
I felt the heart-pounding thinking of where the way would lead
The stress we carry and the words we thought were now far from the league
The talkings and laughs in the place, which act as a beats
A flash from the lane I remember where brought everyone to their feet
A thing like this which people around haven’t felt for a while
I remember those shining eyes in which I kept on looking and was later filled
The laugh I heard was the same as I heard when to daydream ideas cooked
I remember how a simple cutting made them feel something with which he is hooked between
How we and our conversation were of different heights
I remember talking for hours of ones who yet have to call after that fight
All those initials we called each other from, of whom we once have fallen for
I remember.. That jerk too which rushed me through 9 years to the faces which waited across
Seen them wasn’t different but bit peculiar as remember them all,
Looking at each other remember how we looked underneath that hall
Thinking how he uses to laugh and say things, and all those memories we got
The brother with short height and in touch was now a boss in a firm grip
The boy with a beating heart had a girl next to him, holding like no wag to slip
The one with fats hanging off his belt had a built
The one with the real sense but never been serious, she now had attitude hilt
The one who wasn’t confident now had command over what he spoke
The one with the name of a cartoon character was no longer a part of different flock
The one with the sweet smile had dark circles taking over
What I once thought and what I remember were in the past now
All we know is that’s the time we have no matter how
So we did what we are best at
Brought all of the memories around the bonfire and there we sat
