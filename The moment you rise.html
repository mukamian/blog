Resistance to thoughts no longer a task to do
And nothing is channelizing but seems to point at one or two

And to feel it, one had to be through a lot of curls
But success is the only thing which is next you could see
This is those for whom failure is yet another fee
The risk is of losing what you own
But standing at the bottom, rising is the only way can be on…
