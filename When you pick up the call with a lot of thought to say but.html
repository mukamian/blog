A lot of things which left to say
Those thought come up while walking down the way
Wish I could get her here who those are to be conveyed
As over the time those feeling hadn’t ever fade
She think everything I do is to get her close
Now whatever I write have a reflection which do flows
I never wanted her to know what I felt as may lead to mess
But, world I could be blamed for this.. may be less
Wish that day turns back so that I could get that call back
Flashing her name on screen but that time confidence did lack
What to say what not to, the thought were in rush
But her voice was fair enough to make me blush
Word got struck that time when she had call
But now I would tell her whatever had actually made me fall
This time I won’t let my emotions choke
And why can’t I move on I would have spoke
And all the feel from very beginning turns into word
I don’t care and even need them to work
Just hope that understanding may lead to better as friend
Never let her go at least from this end
But irony is in the realization that time had passed
And no matter what I could do now that moment didn’t last
