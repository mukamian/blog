A day which started as normal as possible, but for you
Cause for us, this day wasn’t an ordinary neither the one blowing in a boo
This day, was the relief from the fact that my notebook is still not complete
Or what was going to happen as I had to get my notebook checked or yesterday’s fleet
Or all of this and that… today was the relief from the back curving bags we have to carry
Today was no study day without wishing each other any of merry
So the day of school started with a weird thing that happens annually
When I who use to open the class door was welcomed by someone whom I doubted had seen it closed.
Announced to assemble by the bell which today was ranged manually
And so pockets skinned around the gadgets we lined for bag checking
Some caught, some skipped, we headed to the buses with our besties with us...
Been a fact that all the buses are heading to one direction
The fight was to have a seat of desire with the whole group or a fraction
Now set with the people we know around it begun
Laughing giggling at every light that stops and weaken windows bar where we hung
Our teachers were staring but this time with a smile
But we knew their smile hides the word till the new day is while
So we thought to live in the moment and started to sing loud
Words chosen with so delicacy, that message is conveyed without been considered out
With every reduction in voice reduces the concern of teachers along with the rise in voice
It been hours without them shouting to not to make noise
We never had a thought of where are we heading to
Neither we thought from where we began from
All that mattered now is that journey we had memories of
