Hey there This time, this hi is not to be whispered into her ears.
All these calls you have religiously passed over these years
Don’t know how much you contributed is in this title
But still thank you as this don’t matter
You were employed at a wrong field I guess
you should be at borders near the barricades
haunting down every one who ever stared at the landing
Brutal you could turn our army into preventing every infiltration
You are really a best friend anyone could have, I know
Because you never let your friend stand alone
Like a real spy you would just smell the particular site
Drive million miles spoiling every perfect moment ever might
You knew my chance of standing is none
But you know I never give up because of you as it turns to be fun
Your perfectness never got overshadowed
You were a sniper waiting in the meadow
In the open field feeling the wind and knowing it’s speed
And not letting any bee to hum around but you let the thief seep in
Brought my longest lasting love for her to fade
And letting me know you can win without 3 spades
Thank you to listen all the stories and explanations
Thank you for solving every complication
Thank you for making this sorrow story joy to remember
But still you aren’t done protecting her, they are a numbers
