You, you are a person not an imagination
You are not an experience so neither a hallucination
I remember the time we walk down those lanes
Laughing without an issue or a second thought in brain
Do you remember meeting every morning at place where we always gather?
Talking of day and what if’s and rather’s
Wrote those words which were never had a courage to find their way out
But never forgot those thoughts, those feels which we talked about
Here is the word I don’t really have to tell you
“You are beautiful”, said by many but really known to few
And with the time you would and should change
This is required and varies at every stage
I don’t know why I say all this, but nothing ever was you, girl
I was made to realize those words made you uncomfortable
But girl I promise your insecurity is just frill
You and your words always kept every time every thought sorted
Did noticed your expression when anyone brought it
All I’m left are some words and thoughts, jumbled in someone
And I learned to frame them into one
