I thought of stepping out as things needed to be discussed
And turned out it all made you nothing but disgust
It put on stack what we had
May not so great but not so sad
Now all we left is here, lanced
Now all we have is just a silence
Sometimes loneliness strikes as to ensure
“Are you there alone”, to know for sure
As it turns out, it has been a while now
And this is what I like, but how?
Remember, those lanes we walked chatting and laughing
Those talks have stretched and tensed when our ways departed
Sure, we tried to seek a peek time to time
But so desperate me to phrase them in something which even had no rhymes
You told me what I didn’t anticipate
But this is how I have it interpreted
The awkwardness led to embarrassment
Now embarrassment led to grief
Everything I remembered piled into just a brief
Summing things to a mistake and let it go
This made that call happen in that fall
You told me to move on and how things could go different
But you know what I want someone different
You were the crush of mine when I was young
This makes no sense but I remember all the time that we hung
Whether it be on the first floor where I told you that you would be first to know
Or whether that ground where I stood watching you go
I don’t want someone different
I want things to be different between us
If you think it’s a mistake why don’t you just make one
I don’t know if it will be perfect but I know it would be a better one
