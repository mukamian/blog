Thought to say it all what matters
So found a place stood there half an hour before thinking things are going to be better
She emerges from the crowd
I felt the shiver in my ground
But dedicated like a student waiting I stood
For the result to announce
Started with a wave, our steps were in sync
Now was the time I think
Time went slow, heartbeat went loud
Pressing it in so that she couldn’t hear a sound
I had thought of how it would go
I will say something that she won’t let me go
Thinking, I thought it would be difficult to explain what I told
But the real struggle was to utter something that does not sound cold
But stubborn as a student up on stage, I walked
For telling the tale I thought all-day
I collected myself and words managed to come out
Now when they did there was no world around
It was effortless which made more sense
Love was something we feel and thus no judgment from the fence
Told her the start, cause and reason like any other story
The tale I told had multiple layers as any other multi-stores
But still, it was weird cause she didn’t give a look
and she turned looked and said the most unexpected – “Bye”
Everything shattered into a reality that it was a dream
