The one who rises stretching it’s beauty
Everyday, may be a day just like it is it’s duty
Sails all day to the west and then tired, dozing slowly
Turns colours in its show to fade to lonely
But the coin turns and the scene changes
The one that left still shines in another but varies in ranges
Now it is not the same as the source but still true beauty of its own
Accompanied by a million others on which traveller counts upon
At this moment, every bird out for the day flew back home
The one who’s waiting finds some hopes
Clinging to the builds that stood still
The left out beauty gets filled
The companion sings and dance comes and fades
White something and may be of different shades
As the moment grew, silence stretches too
And the thoughts of anyone and everyone goes through
With every light out begins a journey of the new world
The world of possibilities and all that is left...
