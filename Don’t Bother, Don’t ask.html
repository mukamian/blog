It’s been a while since we met
But now when we do, everything is ready in my head
All the anticipated way the conversation may go
What, how and when to say that would drive conversation high or low
I thought about every situation over and over
Built that anxiety, nervousness, fear over and over
Each word I have thought of, have been crisped
Struggling btw the decision to hang up or pick
Now I have perfect words perfectly timed
With this in my mind, I watch the hands dragging the time
And now is the day we finally met
Things I will say were in my head arranged, all set
So, you walk down with that smile and squeaked eyes
All my thoughts ran under the bus and confidence, well that seems like a lie
Holding me up, I gathered myself
Bringing myself back.
“Hi”
I was ashamed of myself
And I kept waiting for driving the conversation
Each topic that they picked was of some vacation
Now, the sun rushed to the shade
Every thought I had begun to fade
All that was left was just a “bye”
And finally, another word
Don’t bother saying why
