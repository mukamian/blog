So, I thought to put what we had together with a perfect metaphor

But still trying to figure out a way to connect the dots between the twinkle in your eyes with wrinkles next to them just because of your cheeks which puffed after your upper lip decided to hide underneath the shine of your perfect teeth and you know what, your attempt to cover up this mischief by putting your hand over it with the shiny little ring which still hasn’t left a mark on your finger, was no help either.
Thus I met you again in open, but then intervened strand of your hair blown onto your face and you just struggling to cover this mischief too but failing repeatedly, finally… deciding to tie them up but some strands still found a way out, touching your cheeks playfully reminding you, the wind is still blowing and leaving me, still waiting.
But this time I made sure there was nothing, just and I, without those jokes, without that wind, nothing… really nothing, now there we were you and I, with no laughter, with no cheerfulness. Just a long silence. The silence that spread with every fall of the dial. Digging deep, widening. Us, losing hopes of even trying. Just waiting for it to distant, us- again to you and me.
Yeah, I thought of a perfect metaphor. 3AM.
