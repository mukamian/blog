Drew my diary back on my table
Opening it was a risk, as had the label
Attempts to portrait her in the form of rhymes
Which I once wrote but now turns to lime
I thought to never go back in those lanes
But her name is so in mine that something makes me insane
She is not my love as love is a thing which is two ways
But I am into it and I can’t see the shore in this bay
They say that she is too far, and how long you be alone
They don’t know, she never leaves my thoughts, but ever comes along
These are the thing I would have told her someday
With a lot of courage, I reached her to say
But her smile, her eyes made me stay
And I went off quite, walking along her as a friend on the way
