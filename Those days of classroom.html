Always seen your expressions over long hours
Thoughts that how varied they and you are
But now the time is not our
And it leads us to a distance maybe not too far
And the reason to stare is not at all same
Even my staring is been seen with different frame
Things you never said but they do have claimed
Those words made my feeling to set on flames
I never wish to tell you what I feel as it turns to show off
But your face when you read them turned like blew off
So picked up the pen, but now it had no way to turn off
And I write more than what I would ever say off
Girl, I did get appreciation which I had not ever thought
But all happiness was when you got me caught
Your words were like “What you keep on writing” I haven’t forgot
Some day you, on my side, laugh on what I wrote, tight as friends in every sort
