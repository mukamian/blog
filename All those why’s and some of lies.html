Girl, I know it doesn’t always make sense to you
But here I am giving it off, and you know exactly for whom
You are the only one whom I had always been thinking about
All the time being particular about what I wrote or spoke out
Friends, you told me we always going to be and that is what we are
But still, think of this… and tell me Why are we still so far?
Every time things go wrong I accept, it was always something that I have done
But never you ever think or even talked about why I did to be the one
You told me you like to be a mystery
But every moment I met you I feel I had turned like a history
Now things went so wrong that we don’t have a topic to talk on
And all those small things that matter you, had turned upon
I don’t know why you can say those things to her but can’t turn to me for all
You once told me I wasn’t the problem and I even felt that it had to solve
Everything you told me, I had tossed them all
Every word you told now seemed to turn up lime
And I know if I asked you… “Everything is fine”
But why don’t you understand things thought of, are told so that you never hesitate to do the same
And five years I had admired you and all I left is this shame
But it is not the first time I told you this
And I still believe you will misunderstand as things might have gone amiss
But girl please whatever you think of me say it on my face
Might be the miscommunication which led our friendship to this phase
