A life like I wish could be mine
A day whose memory is for miles
A morning with discomfort but still a smile
A lot of deeds to say but don’t know what happened in this while
Sun shows his last shine for the day
And gloom seems to be in its way
All excitement had turned to hay
As my face tells the same story as they
A known voice asks how was the day
And the long stories of mine begins when they say
Kept on to convey and never realized to ask them back
And this time that is the thing which lack
Now the time had changed things grew old
Today I have also not the same but still, I am bold
Bold enough to sustain whatever they told
But listening to them no one asks what my thoughts hold
Everyone’s storyline is same and now it bores
No one tells good happenings but keep crying at fauls
Things do go wrong but no need of such hauls
And now I gained my wits back
Some sound now surrounds like in lakhs
Snores all around me I had felt
The other thing that day had left
