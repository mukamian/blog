On the way led by the headlight
Driving with the moon beside
Cool breeze adds to fact of being alone
Somewhere into that dark where the winds haul in the wood
You, next to me leaned your head on me in comfort
As if you know I am frightened
Frightened of “Don’t know”, “what if” and even every headlight passing by
But you, next to me how secured you felt
Though in that dark, drive deep into the nowhere
To believe that this night would lead us to the place where we could live like we
You are not just a woman I love or care or fight for
You are someone who brings me up who’s care and dependence help me find home
When I drove miles into doing something…. WRONG
You don’t make everything in my life right
But I need you by me when the things aren’t in our way
You are beautiful and the reason I remember to think of the person you love
Without you, I won’t be lost but neither would have found myself
I don’t know where to lead this further and how to describe
Can’t even ask you to stay forever because as that should be served as a choice…
