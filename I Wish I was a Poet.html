Sometimes I wish if I was a poet
So, I could pearl every falling drop into a string to let her know I care. I care for her so much that I wish this rain keeps falling and be the reason for her to stay.
So, I could rhyme every word every line that describes her. Rhyme would show the symphony on her face when she needs to know she is beautiful.
So, I could feel every emotion that flows down her face. Feel every emotion when she couldn’t explain, I could be there to tell her, “I understand”.
So, I could have a perfect metaphor for every imperfection she has. A metaphor to show the perfect sync of the imperfections when she questions her worth.
So, I could be known for the resonance of my word with her. Resonance to keep me awake at night with the thought that she never left and to feel things as they were.
