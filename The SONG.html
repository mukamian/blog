I have a playlist that changes every dawn
They have been there at every happiness and frown
Listening to them started with whatever feels nice
Leading to what they thought was good and thus naive
But soon the words found the way to understanding
Clearing the fog and soon I stopped pretending
They lingered to the thoughts and started to relate
Clinging to what happened, what she said and began to paint
Now whenever a song plays on a loop
It breaks into words, memory and just lay in a hoop
Listening to them is just a ride back home
Defaulting the guards and decreasing the value of ohms
Some remind of someone, somewhere, some times
And others just stays waiting for their own
SOMEONE, SOMEWHERE, SOMETIMES.
